General purpose application

A general purpose application, sometimes known as 'off-the-shelf' is the sort of software that you use at home and school. General general purpose applications are cheaper than Tailor-made applications.

Types of General Purpose Apllication Software

  1. Stand Alone Programs

    2. It is fully self-contained and requires a lot of storage space.

  2. Integrated Programs

    3. Packages that combine the features of several applications into one software package.Less powerful than stand alone programs.

  3. Software Suite

    4. Collection of individual programs sold as a single package. Work together and offer the same command & structures.

Examples of General general purpose application

  1. Productivity

    2. Example: World, PowerPoint and Excel
    These apps make users become more efficient and effective during their work.

  2. Comminication

    3. Examples: Facebook, Instagram and Twitter.
    Software that allows users to communicate and get connected with other users.

  3. Media

    4. Examples: Image editing app, video editing app and photo editing app.
    These apps create and edit graphical design or multimedia
