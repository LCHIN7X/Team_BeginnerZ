What is Financial Literacy

Understanding Financial Literacy

-Financial literacy is the ability to understand and effectively use various financial skills,
-including personal financial management, budgeting, and investing.

-When you are financially literate, you have the essential foundation for a smart relationship with money.
-his can help start a lifelong journey of learning about the financial aspects of your life.
-The earlier you start to become financially literate, the better off you'll be because education is the key to a successful financial future.
