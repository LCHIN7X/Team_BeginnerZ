Get Your Credit Card Under Control

Managing credit card debt is key to financial security—yet many older adults can’t make ends meet without putting daily expenses on a charge card.

It can be difficult for older adults on fixed incomes to pay down balances. As a result, mounting credit card debt becomes a tremendous stressor.

While it can be challenging, it is possible to get credit card debt under control. These 5 key strategies can help.

5 key strategies to help you get your credit card debt under control

  1. Contact your credit card companies
  2. Understand the two ways to pay off credit card debt
  3. Consider a debt management plan
  4. Participate in credit counseling
  5. Get your money muscles in peak shape
